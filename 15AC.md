## [Aggressive Cows & https://takeuforward.org/data-structure/aggressive-cows-detailed-solution]

**Rating:** (Hard)
**Topic:** (Binary Search)
**Pattern:** (Logic + Binary Search)

### 🧠 The "Aha!" Moment (The Logic)

_You are given an array 'arr' of size 'n' which denotes the position of stalls. You are also given an integer 'k' which denotes the number of aggressive cows._
_You are given the task of assigning stalls to 'k' cows such that the minimum distance between any two of them is the maximum possible. Find the maximum possible minimum distance._

_Ex: "We needed to sort out our array then we had to find the minimum distance respectively from the lowest element to the most possible element."_

### ⚠️ The Trap (Where did I get stuck?)

- "The minimum possible maximum is a different parttern of problem than just finding a minimum or maximum element."

### ⚡ Complexity

- **Time:** O(N \* log N) + O(N) \* O(log(N))
- **Space:** O(1)
